‘’Binti Shujaa Project’’ focuses on addressing three key strategic areas, first school re-entry
program for adolescence mothers, Capacity building program for out of schools adolescents
female and Resilience of female adolescents towards teenage pregnancy. Project designed
in a strategic and intergrated way towards finding a sustainable solutions for female
adolescents. Teenage pregnancy exposes adolescent women to the serious threats in social,
economic and medical aspect. They are in high risks of dying in child birth, risk of living in
poverty as single mothers, risks of death during illegal abortion and social exclusion as
well. This program will cover three strategic dimensions above to create a synergic impacts
