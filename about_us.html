About Our Charity

FOUNDATION FOR GIRL’S HOPE – (FGH) is a child centred, Non-government Organization registered 2019 under the Non-governmental Organizations Act 2002. Given registration No. ooNGO/R/096 to work in Tanzania mainland. The organization focuses on the protection of children and elimination of all forms of abuse and stigmatization to children, advocating issues of children in the community and awareness raising on basic children’s rights, gender equality and equity. Before formal registration in July 2019, Foundation for Girls Hope started as a community group which focused on championing children’s rights and protection especially on education right to children and young girls. Group mainly operated in Maasai communities on raising awareness trying to break the cultural bound which are hindrance factor towards upbringing education right to children. Achievement made on emphasing education rights and reporting all forms of abuse and discrimination, geared the advancement to an NGO so that to expand the vision inorder to reach many children and young girls.

ORGANIZATION STRATEGIC AREAS

FOUNDATION FOR GIRL’S HOPE ORGANIZATION have four (4) strategic areas where these four strategic areas comprises nine (9) strategic programs objectives in all of its workplan. These four areas as follows;

STRATEGIC AREA 1: CHILDREN’S RIGHTS AND PROTECTION PROGRAM
⦁The 4 Rs Physco Social Support (Rescue, Rehabilitation, Resilience and Reallocation) Program.

In collaboration with community structures and key community actors, program will deal with rescue children from Abuse, Offer guidance and councelling to abused children, they will be capacitated to adopt to different situation and giving a new home to a child/children when necessary. Foundation for Girls Hope aiming to establish a strong Bond with Social Walfar e-departments, getting on the ground to support children who have experienced abuse either financial support/PSS support, link and refferral etc. This will depend on case by case reported. FGH will budget a standby emergence fund for the services. Though there will be a continued role of advocating the promotion of children right and protection, to ensure all children are safe, enjoy their rights and far better to ensure all children are free from harm.

⦁Safe Space Education Program.

This program intends to prevent children from Online Violance/Abuse. Recently, there is an increases number of children and young people who are using multi social media platforms. This wave of use is highly triggered by development of technology, availability and axcess of digital devices. Adolescent are likely to interact with computers, phones, tablets and other electronic device in everyday life at home and in the school environment. It is the matter of fact, this interactions cannot be avoided and adolescent users are not safe as well. Therefore children/young boys and girls must be guided on how they can be in a safe side while online, by considering number of things like building a positive online reputation and maintaining privacy etc. If not well are not guided on how to use them positively, likely children might fall the risks of abuse.

STRATEGIC AREA 2: EDUCATION.
⦁ SEXUAL REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH EDUCATION PROGRAM.
⦁ Awareness to Children/Adolescent on Growth and Development

The purpose of this program is to mentor children, so that they can develop required and necessary desirable skills needed to cope with voluntary and involuntary natural character associated with development of human being. Children will learn the transition path from childhood to adolecent stage and its characters. This goes hand to hand with the rights entitled to adolecent and what to do when their rights are violated. Stages of adolescent growth and development more likely character associated with physical development/puberty. Again they will be learning anatomy of reproductive system (both male and female), Children will be equipped with knowledge of social and emotional changes during adolescence. These are some of the key issues that will be touched in stages of growth and development. Program will extend to cover the folowing as well-; Adolescent Vs sexuality and adolescent Vs STI’s: children will be capacitated with skills on how to delay sexual activities (abstinence) and its numerous benefits like avoiding STI’s, early pregnancies etc. so that they can succeed to promote their self-worth and self-esteem. Adolescent Vs reproductive organs diseases: like cervical cancers, UTI’s (Urinary Tract Infections), to learn the risk factors/causes and how to prevent reproductive organ diseases, proceduces to follow on doing examination and necessary vaccinations reguired per established medical standards. Adolescent Vs Drugs use and abuse: Adolescent group is at high risk of drugs uses due to peers pressure and some have fallen the risks, this program intends to help children on how to avoid risk behaviours and providing measures/asistance to enable help those who have fallen the risks already.

⦁Menstrual Health and Hygiene Practice (MHH).

Through this program children (especially girls) will learn important changes occuring in their body with the start of puberty: The menstrual cycle, how to manage their first monthly period (menarche), How to use and dispose the pads, how to keep oneself clean during period, how to manage headache and stomach pain during monthly period, also program will inspire girls to have someone in their inner trust cycle in school or at home for sharing information incase there is a challenges require necessary support.

3-Main activities will be done under MHHP component:

Installing menstrual care system in schools:Program has prepared separate proposal plans, aiming to build special changing rooms in schools, not simply toilets as we have experienced on the grounds rather its full accommodate many supportive facilities including; emergence bed rest-double deca with capacity of taking 15-20 children at once, first aid kits, emergence sanitary pads, 2-washing toilets 1-bath room, 1-changing room within as well as installed incinerator machine outside for burning used pads.

Training ToT to make re-usable pad: selection will be done among the students in schools. This will serve two purposes (health safety and economic). Firstly its known some children are coming from under resourced families, many can’t afford purchasing sanitary pads every month to support their girl’s children, alternative is required and far better considering safety as local made reusable pads proven to be safe.

Awareness raising sessions among the Children and Youth: This will be done on the practices of Menstrual hygiene by sharing information, initiate menstrual hygiene discussions among girls and boys to learn the challenges and struggle that girls are facing. Also to capacitate teachers to support students as well as empower different peers with the tasks of inspiring others to be self confident, maintaining clearnness and act respectful regardless of environment.

This awareness is purposely aiming to strengthening gender role, so that male teachers and boys students can offer their support to young girls instead of creating environment which stigmatize girls and feeling down.

Its ine vitable for youth to be in peers or groups as its part and percel of enhancing their emotional and social health. But if guidance measures are not emphasized, there is a high risks of children to join wrong peer groups.

The program helps to builds a child’s ability in for important areas which stand as foundations, First children to be able to identify (Talent or Gift), Secondly having unaltered positive attitude influenced by environment.Thirdly believing in uniqueness and Lastly building right Network, especially with those with similar vision/objective.

⦁ HEALTH CARE AND NUTRITION PROGRAM.

This is one of the most important aspect to learn, many children and youth have insufficient knowledge of what healthcare is all about in general and what impact (positive or negative) do they have in our body and LIFE in general. FGH is focusing to incurbate young girls and boys to inventing and live a culture of being concious in healthcare and nutrition related issue. Through this program, young people will be invited to take challenge by increasing their knowledge, strength and skills in many areas of emotional health, Nutrition and physical exercise, Environment and disease prevention. In nutshell the overview of these aspects are the following;

⦁ Nutrition and physical activity

Growth during adolescence is faster than at any time in child’s life time. Iron requirement also increases dramatically as the result of increase in total blood volume, body muscle. Healthy eating during adolescence is important as the body changes affect adolescent’s nutritional and dietary needs. Therefore children will be empowered on the benefits of good nutrition, how to overcome unhealthy eating habits, basic understands of nutritions founds in varieties of food inorder to make better informed choice of what to eat, in this program we are going to use this slogan (what you eat is whom you become). In second aspect, children and youth must be tought how physical activity strengthen their immunity and builds their self-esteem and far better the importance of practicing (taking physical excercise).

⦁ Environment aspect.

Emphasis on environmental issues has yet cuptured the minds of many young people, due to the fact that many addressing environment issues separatetly from the LIFE of human being. This perception itselfs is wrong. What we have used to call environment and human being share a single system. As environment affect our health, we do as the same to the enviroment. Food we eat, air we breath and all other human needs we depends from the environment but the global practices of human activities can leave the earth better or worse in each day, environment do to us as well. Through this program, apart from helping children and youth changing perceptions but also many will participate in practical activities that will help them to make a good life choices, where from the childhood, they will slowly changing their culture and become better environment protectors/activists in the future.

⦁ Mental Health:

Emotional, psychological, spiritual and social well-being are what makes a human being in a completeness of his/her nature. Mental health determines how young people can handle challenges, how to relate to others and make informed choices. Children and adolecent needs good mental health to develop in a healthy way, building strong relationships and adapt to changes and deal with life challenges. Inevitable challenges might cause stress sometimes, therefore knowledge of stress management is required. Stress management: signs of stress, causes of stress, how to avoid and manage stress in general will be covered in these series of learning. Stress can be avoided and little stress is beneficial, However too much stress can be harmful, unhealth and exhausting for a child/youth both physcal and psychological. This program focus on helping children to have minimum standard capacity of dealing with stress, due to the fact that people who use stress management strategies are better able to cope with the period of stress. Children under FGH programs will be empowered inorder to maintain strong programs of personal wellness and be able to cope with daily irritations and problems which are inevitable. The detailed materials will be provided to different learning platform in a simplified and friendly version fit the children in their level

⦁ Drug use/abuse, Alcohol and Tobacco/Marijuana:

Many people including (youth and children) believes drugs can cure diseases, but this is not entirely truth. Drugs can only help the body in its efforts, as all drugs has far reaching chemical effects on the body. Only the body can cure diseases in its origin/natural processes. There is a fine line between correct and incorrect use of drugs. They must be effective, but also be safe, thats why this program needs to capacitate children with relevant knowledge and skills regarding the matter. Also there is a growing number of alcohol, sigarette and marijuana users, who are children and youth both girls and boys. There is a high need of program target group to be informed why some have fallen the risks of excessive use, health effects and strategies for recovery if one is addicted.

⦁ Disease prevention

We are all surrounded by millions of microbes living things which are too small for human eyes to see without the help of microscope. A microscopic zoo lives all around us- on the surfaces we touch, in the air we breath on the food that we lift to our mouth as well as on the surfaces of our body. Most are harmless and perfoms valuable services, However can cause infectious diseases. These microbes known as pathogens. Its important for children to learn how the body defences itself, what action to take to maintain high immunity. The body has many barriers against infectious diseases. If diseases does get past the first barrier, the body can often still fight it off. What are those barriers? Children shall be informed. Also there is a point of VIRUSES which differ greatly from bacteria, its important to know different forms of viruses and how they can be transmitted, cured and treated as well. Therefore children under FGH programs will be empowered and offered with strategies they can use to protect themselves against infections.

⦁ Safety, Emergence and First aid:

⦁ Safety, Emergence and First aid: Safety: getting to know how: Its important to ask yourself this question “How safety concious you are??” Its necessary for youth to learn how to be careful enough to avoid getting hurt and lossing joy in life. SAFETY is key in many aspects of life. Sometimes accidents are anavoidable, thus children shall be prepared with natural disasters when happens, and what proper action to take in the middle of trouble.

Emergence and first aid: children must be tought in emergence situation what to do, as fast effective action can save a life, perhaps their own life as children. Many people dies in accidents needlessly because no one nearby knows how to help effective. Children under our program will be equiped with basic knowledge of emergence measures. Howcver understanding basic knowledge and reading can not adequately preparing them to be a real help in an emergence situations. Most attempt to help without right knowledge can do more harm than good, therefore FGH will be preparing shortcouses training so that children will be able to take class from qualified instructors who teach First aid in proffessional bases.

STRATEGIC AREA 3: PROMOTE FAMILY CARE AND POSITIVE PARENTING (TRIPPLE ‘P’)
⦁ Promote Positive Parenting

Parents are among the most important people in the lives of children and youth. Parents including mothers and fathers as well as other caregivers who are guardians. But in most cases parents lack information and the appropriate support that they need for good and effective parenting. Though parents have diverse needs, therefore no single approach is right for all parents. For effectively supporting children, parents engagement is key in the journey of success. For effective engagement and cooperation, programs designed in such a ways that treating parents as equal partners when figuring out which services most benefit them and their children

⦁ Family Care component:

Every child needs the nurturing support of family and the experience of community in order o thrive. Family is the most important sources of love, emotional support, material sustainance and moral guidance in a child’s life. As an organization we are committed to offer constructive programs that will contribute on helping to ensure children remains in appropriate family care. Programs will suit the diverse cultures of families, create network opportunities for parents to connect and receive supports from other parents with similar circumstances and ensure families with multiple service needs receive coordinated services.

Organization has a future project ‘’TUWALEE NYUMBANI’’ This will put emphasis on different community care settings for children; there are children who cared under family of origin, in-extended families, in-foster families and residential care. Upon the start of this project focus will be on advocacy towards strengthening family care of different kinds, in order to enhance development by nurturing children within a commuity family care setting.
